Soaring Higher!

Last year, Scouting America witnessed the end of a tradition with the termination of Journey to Excellence. The Scouters in the Talakto District voiced their frustration with this decision, and the District leadership developed a new way to measure the health of our units in the district.

Enter Soaring Higher!

"Soaring Higher!" is the new tool of measurement for Units to plan their annual activities. There is a three-tiered system of awards: the Red Feather (lowest level), White Feather (middle level), and Blue Feather (top level). When a unit succeeds in achieving one of these levels, it will be awarded a patch with the level feather on it.

Designed to help every unit to continue on the journey of excellence, with this new tool of measurement, units are given the opportunity to be Soaring Higher!
